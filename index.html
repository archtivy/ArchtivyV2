Projects, products, credits & files for architecture.

Archtivy connects built projects with the products and people behind them. Credits link teams to projects; product files and discovery by location make the network useful for specifiers and brands.

Explore projects Explore products Start demo tour

Why it works

  • Credits — Projects and products attribute real people and studios, so work is discoverable and verifiable.
  • Product files — Spec sheets, 3D models, and CAD/BIM assets live next to products; members can download for specification.
  • Discovery by location — Find nearby projects and see what products were used, so local context drives decisions.

What’s next

  • Real auth — Magic links and verified accounts so credits and downloads are tied to real identities.
  • Verified profiles — Claim and verify your profile so studios and brands can trust credits and file access.
  • Full map explore — Optional map view for projects and products by region for discovery at scale.
